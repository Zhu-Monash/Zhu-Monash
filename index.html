Australian health expenditure by area and source

Government expenditure in each state

In terms of expenditure in all states in Australia, New South Wales has the highest proportion, reaching 32.6%. This is followed by Victoria with 24.5% Queensland was third with 19.6%. These three states accounted for 76% of total expenditures

Expenditure trends (1997 to 2011)

NSW expenditures have always accounted for a relatively high proportion of total Australian government expenditures, and the gap with the second and third states has maintained a steady upward trend.

Expenditure Area

The top three expenditure areas are public hospital, medical services, and private hospital, highlighting the government's emphasis on people's health care

Expenditure category

In the category of government expenditure, Australian government expenditure accounts for the majority. State government or local government is relatively small. Especially in the field of healthcare, most of them are government-led
